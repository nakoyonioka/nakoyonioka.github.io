Project Overview

This multi-channel biopotential amplifier is designed for physiological signal acquisition, built around AD8421ARZ instrumentation amplifiers. Engineered with features for patient safety , with digital isolation, and signal conditioning , this design incorporates an analog front-end for biomedical applications. Key features include a Driven Right Leg (DRL) option for Channel 1, multi-stage filtering (0.5Hz HPF, 50Hz Notch, 70Hz LPF) per channel, and an AD7682BCP ADC.

Technical Deep Dive

Signal Chain Architecture

Electrode Interface

Differential inputs for each channel. Channel 1 includes an option for DRL (Driven Right Leg) or virtual ground connection. ESD protection diodes (BAV199 type) are present on inputs. An audio connector (J34) can be used for electrode cables.

Audio Connector Option (J34) DRL Option (Channel 1) ESD Protection (BAV199 type)

Analog Front-End (AFE)

Each channel uses an AD8421ARZ instrumentation amplifier. Subsequent stages use AD8606ARMZ-R7 op-amps for filtering and gain. Channel 1 has a specified gain of 101 in its final op-amp stage (U7B). Channels 2, 3, and 4 have a specified gain of 23 in their corresponding final op-amp stages (U14B, U21B, U28B).

AD8421ARZ INA AD8606ARMZ-R7 Op-Amps Channel Specific Gain Stages

Signal Conditioning

Each channel incorporates a 0.5Hz high-pass filter, a 50Hz notch filter (with bypass jumpers), and a 70Hz low-pass filter.

0.5Hz HPF 50Hz Notch Filter (Bypassable) 70Hz LPF

ADC & Isolation

The analog signals are digitized by an AD7682BCP ADC. Digital signals (SPI) are isolated using an ISO7341Q digital isolator. Jumpers are available for SPI selection (local or isolated).

AD7682BCP ADC ISO7341Q Isolator SPI Interface

Signal Integrity

Instrumentation amplifiers (AD8421ARZ) for initial signal amplification
Differential inputs for common-mode noise rejection
REF3025 (2.5V) precision voltage reference used for the analog front end (Vdrive) and ADC reference (REFIN)
AD7682BCP ADC for signal conversion
Op-amp bypass capacitors (typically 100nF) are used throughout the design

Patient Safety Features

Galvanic isolation of SPI signals via ISO7341Q digital isolator
Designed for battery-powered operation, featuring BQ29723 battery protection IC
Driven Right Leg (DRL) option on Channel 1 to reduce common-mode interference
ESD protection diodes (BAV199 type) on input lines

Noise Rejection

Multi-stage filtering: 0.5Hz HPF, 50Hz Notch, 70Hz LPF per channel
Bypass jumpers provided for the 50Hz notch filter
"Vdrive" (buffered 2.5V reference from REF3025) used as a stable reference for analog stages

Power Management

BQ29723 battery protection IC for safe battery operation, including overvoltage (OVP), undervoltage (UVP), overcurrent (OCD), and short circuit (SCD) protection features mentioned in its typical application context
TPS61230DRC boost converter to generate +5V from battery input
LM2776 charge pump to generate -5V from the +5V rail
REF3025 precision 2.5V voltage reference (Vdrive) for analog circuitry
