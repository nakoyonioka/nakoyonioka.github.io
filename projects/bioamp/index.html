Project Overview

This multi-channel biopotential amplifier showcases cutting-edge biomedical engineering built around high-precision AD8421ARZ instrumentation amplifiers. Engineered for patient safety and clinical-grade signal quality, this design represents the perfect fusion of analog front-end design and medical device engineering.

Technical Specifications
