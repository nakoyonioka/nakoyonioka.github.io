PCB Design From Scratch

Learn Professional PCB Design Techniques Using KiCad

Welcome to the PCB Design From Scratch course! Printed Circuit Boards (PCBs) are the foundation of almost all modern electronic devices. Learning to design your own PCBs is a crucial skill for any hardware engineer, allowing you to create compact, reliable, and professional-looking electronic projects.

This course will guide you through the entire PCB design process using KiCad, a powerful, open-source EDA (Electronic Design Automation) suite. From schematic capture to final manufacturing files, you'll learn industry best practices and gain the confidence to bring your electronic ideas to life on a custom board.

Course Modules:

Module 1: Schematic Capture

Designing your circuit diagram

Learn to create clean, readable schematics, select components, and connect them logically in KiCad.

Module 2: Component Footprints

Linking symbols to physical parts

Understand how to associate schematic symbols with their corresponding physical footprints for PCB layout.

Module 3: Board Layout

Routing traces and placement

Dive into the core of PCB design: component placement, trace routing, and design rule checking for manufacturability.

Module 4: Gerber Generation

Preparing for manufacturing

Learn how to generate the necessary manufacturing files (Gerbers) and prepare your design for a PCB fabrication house.
