Analog Circuits Workshop

Understanding Electronics Fundamentals through Practical Builds

Welcome to the Analog Circuits Workshop! This series is designed to demystify the world of analog electronics by guiding you through the construction of practical circuits. Analog circuits are the backbone of many electronic devices, dealing with continuous signals like voltage, current, and frequency.

Each module focuses on a core analog concept, providing theoretical background, detailed circuit diagrams, component lists, and troubleshooting advice. By building these circuits, you'll gain hands-on experience and a deeper intuition for how electronic components behave in the real world.

Workshop Modules:

Module 1: Voltage Dividers

Basic resistive networks

Understand how to scale voltages using simple resistor networks, a fundamental building block in countless analog designs.

Module 2: Transistor Amplifier

Single-stage audio amp

Explore the magic of transistors by building a basic amplifier circuit, learning about gain, biasing, and signal amplification.

Module 3: Op-Amp Circuits

Practical applications

Dive into the versatile world of Operational Amplifiers, building circuits like inverting/non-inverting amplifiers, buffers, and summing amplifiers.

Module 4: Active Filters

Low-pass and high-pass design

Learn to design and build active filters that shape signal frequencies, essential for audio processing and sensor conditioning.
