Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 22, 2025

This Privacy Policy outlines how Ana's Portfolio ("we," "us," or "our") handles information collected from visitors to our website.

1. Information We DO NOT Collect

We are committed to maintaining your privacy. This website does not directly collect any personal information about its visitors. We do not use tracking technologies like cookies or pixels to gather data about your Browse habits, IP address, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. Contact Form (Third-Party Service)

Our website features a contact form to allow you to get in touch with us. This form is powered by a third-party service called Formspree. When you submit a message through the contact form:

  • The information you provide (such as your name, email address, and message content) is sent directly to us via Formspree.
  • Formspree acts as a data processor for these submissions. We encourage you to review Formspree's own Privacy Policy to understand how they handle data submitted through their service.
  • We will only use the information you provide in your message to respond to your inquiry. We do not store this information on our website's servers, nor do we share it with any other third parties, beyond the necessary processing by Formspree.

3. External Links

Our website may contain links to other websites that are not operated by us (e.g., project links, social media profiles). We have no control over and assume no responsibility for the content, privacy policies, or practices of any third-party sites or services.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the contact form on our website.
