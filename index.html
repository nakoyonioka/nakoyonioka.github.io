About Me

Hardware Engineer & DIY Electronics Enthusiast

With a Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering and an M.S. in Electronic & Computer Engineering, I've spent the past seven years developing embedded systems and industrial hardware. My expertise spans STM32, Raspberry Pi, ESP32, and 8051 microcontrollers, with a focus on creating robust, production-ready electronics.

My professional journey began with designing an innovative in-wheel motor and STM32-based control system for electric bicycles. Since then, I've worked on safety-critical systems for railway signaling (SIL-2/SIL-4 certified), developed ROS2-based robotics platforms, and implemented AI solutions for edge computing and computer vision applications.

I'm passionate about sharing knowledge and helping others break into the world of electronics and hardware. This drives my commitment to creating accessible resources that simplify complex concepts and guide aspiring engineers through practical, hands-on projects.
