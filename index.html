About Me

Hello, I'm Ana!

I am a passionate electronics and hardware engineer with a deep interest in embedded systems design and PCB development. With a Bachelor's in Electrical Engineering and an M.S. in Electronic & Computer Engineering, I've spent the past seven years developing embedded systems and industrial hardware. My expertise spans STM32, Raspberry Pi, ESP32, and 8051 microcontrollers, with a focus on creating robust, production-ready electronics.

With a strong foundation in both theoretical principles and practical applications I enjoy tackling complex challenges. My professional journey began with designing an innovative in-wheel motor and STM32-based control system for electric bicycles. Since then, I've worked on safety-critical systems for railway signaling (SIL-2/SIL-4 certified), developed ROS2-based robotics platforms, and implemented AI solutions for edge computing and computer vision applications.
